About Precon America

Precon America is a Miami-based consulting firm dedicated to enhancing pre-construction and project management processes for construction companies, developers, and public institutions.

Our Mission

We provide proven tools and strategies that enable construction firms, developers, and public entities to reduce costs by up to 10%, secure more project awards, and improve overall profitability. Our approach emphasizes transparency, cutting-edge technology, regulatory compliance, and tailored solutions that make every client more efficient.

Our Principal

Led by a principal with over 18 years of international experience and a track record of successful large-scale projects across South America, Central America, the Caribbean, and the United States. Holding a Master's degree in Project Management (with honors) and a Civil Engineering degree validated in multiple countries, our leadership has consistently delivered exceptional outcomes through expertise in pre-construction and strategic project delivery.
