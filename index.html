About Us

Javier Balza, MBA, is a seasoned civil engineer with over 15 years of experience in large-scale projects ranging from $5MM to $400MM. As the founder of PRECON AMERICA LLC, based in Florida, Javier brings expertise in pre-construction processes, project management, and cost control. Our Services

Pre-Construction Process

Identify project requirements, define scope, coordinate stakeholders, conduct feasibility studies, select methods and materials, develop construction strategy.

Budget Process

Calculate execution costs, quantify materials, quote suppliers, analyze unit prices, estimate indirect costs, define contingencies and profits.

Bidding and Contracting Processes

Use systems like Procore for transparent bidding, create procurement schedules, select qualified vendors, hold alignment meetings, compare bids, sign contracts.

Cost Control and Change Orders

Monitor costs against budget, track plan changes, manage change orders, verify timelines and resources, produce reports, mitigate risks, document lessons learned.

Opportunity Zones Development

Plan projects to foster economic development in IRS-designated opportunity zones, stimulating growth and job creation in low-income communities.
